Traffic Court Online

The tool for resolving your Violation Ticket issues

Begin the process by initiating a dispute. You will be guided by a self resolution wizard.

If you are not currently signed in with your BC Services card, you will first be asked sign in or register.


BC Services Card

Set up your BC Services Card if you haven't done so already (this is a one-time process).

Self Resolution Wizard

Once your BC Services Card is setup, understand the process by following the self resolution wizard.

Violation Ticket Resolution

Upon finishing the self resolution wizard, your ticket should be resolved or ready to be disputed in court.
