BC Traffic Court Online

The online tool for submitting and resolving your BC Violation Ticket disputes

If you are not currently signed in with your BC Services card, you will first be asked sign in or go through the BC Services card registration process.

If you would like to simply pay for your ticket, please click on the 'Pay Ticket (PayBC)' button.

Initiate Dispute

Login using your BC Services card and initiate the online resolution process.

Violation Ticket Lookup

Find your Violation Ticket and ensure that the specifics of the ticket are correct. Through our step-by-step process, you can indicate your preferences and how you would like the ticket to be disputed.

Violation Ticket Resolution

Submit your resolution or save the details until you are ready to proceed with the resolution process. You have 365 days to dispute your ticket from the date the ticket was issued.

Self Resolution Wizard

Interact with an online wizard and explore reasons behind your ticket. The online wizard will take you through multiple options demonstrated through text, videos and images.
