British Columbia ticket resolution
Use this service to pay ticket fines, ask for fine reductions or time to pay, or initiate a dispute. Enter violation ticket information, or upload image of ticket to begin. {{ accordionItem.expanded ? ' - Hide information' : ' + See more information' }}
The types of tickets you can resolve are:
  • Handwritten tickets(start with A): upload an image or take photo of your ticket to create your online ticket.
  • E-tickets (start with E): enter the ticket number and violation time or upload image to find your ticket.
  • Intersection Safety Camera tickets (start with S): enter the ticket number and violation time or upload an image to find your ticket.
