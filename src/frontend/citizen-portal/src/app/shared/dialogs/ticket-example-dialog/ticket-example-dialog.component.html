Ticket types

What letters does your ticket start with? That will tell you your ticket type:

A is a Motor Vehicle Act - Violation Ticket

E is an Electronic Motor Vehicle Act - Violation Ticket

ST or SG is a Motor Vehicle Act - Intersection Safety Violation Ticket (Red light and Speed)

Ticket example

Motor Vehicle Act Violation ticket

This ticket is a handwritten paper ticket, you will receive a blue carbon copy from the police officer. The ticket number starts with an A.

Electronic Motor Vehicle Act Violation ticket

This ticket is an electronic generated ticket, you will receive a white printed copy from the police officer. The ticket number starts with an E.

Motor Vehicle Act - Intersection Safety Violation Ticket (Intersection and red light tickets)

This ticket is an electronic generated ticket, you will receive a white printed copy in the mail. The ticket number can starts with ST or SG.
