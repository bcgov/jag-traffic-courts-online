Image upload information and requirements

Upload an image of your ticket to see if it's already in the system.

If you do not get a ticket search result

If you have a handwritten ticket that starts with an A, you need to create your online ticket. Upload an image of your ticket to start. Follow the prompts.

If you have an Intersection Safety Camera or eTicket and you do not get a result with the information on your ticket, contact the Violation Ticket Centre at 1-888-661-8026.

If you lost your ticket

If you have lost your physical ticket, you must phone the police detachment you received the ticket from to get a copy of your ticket.

For best ticket photo scanning results, follow these instructions:

  • Use a brightly lit area
  • Take your photo on a plain, untextured background
  • Crop your image as close to the edges as possible
  • Ensure image is legible (no blurry text)

Image specifications

  • Acceptable formats: JPG, PNG or PDF
  • Maximum size: 2 MB

NOTE: Please ensure your image meets these requirements or it may be rejected.
